About soil testing

What is Soil Testing?

Soil testing is a process that involves analyzing soil samples to determine their physical, chemical, and biological properties. These tests provide valuable information about the composition, nutrient content, pH levels, organic matter, and other characteristics of the soil. Soil testing helps farmers, gardeners, and land managers make informed decisions about soil management and crop or plant nutrition
